Find the first circular tour that visits all petrol pumps

Input:
1. Number of nodes: n = 4 (A, B, C, D)
2. The amount of petrol that every petrol pump has: [4, 6, 7, 4]
3. Distance from that petrol pump to next next petrol pump: [6, 5, 3, 5]

Output: B-C-D-A-B

Extension: Number of circular tours that visit all petrol pumps
